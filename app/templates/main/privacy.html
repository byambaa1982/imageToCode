1. Introduction

Screenshot to Code ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered screenshot-to-code conversion service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • Username (optional display name)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe, not stored by us)

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Usage statistics (pages visited, features used)
  • Conversion history (framework choices, processing times)

2.3 Uploaded Content

When you use our service:

  • Screenshots are temporarily processed for code generation
  • Generated code is stored in your account history
  • Images are automatically deleted after processing completion

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Account management and authentication
  • Processing screenshots through our AI models
  • Generating and delivering code results
  • Managing credits and billing
  • Providing customer support

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features
  • Improving AI model accuracy with aggregated data
  • Optimizing performance and user experience
  • Developing new features and capabilities

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third parties who help us operate our service:

  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Cloud hosting providers for infrastructure
  • Email service providers for notifications
  • Analytics services for usage insights

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law:

  • Legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Government requests for national security
  • Protection of rights and safety
  • Fraud investigation and prevention

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers and infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Vulnerability monitoring and response

5.2 Image Processing Security

  • Temporary storage only during processing
  • Automatic deletion after code generation
  • Secure transmission to AI processing services
  • No permanent image storage on our servers

6. Data Retention

6.1 Account Data

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive accounts: May be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Account deletion: User can request immediate deletion

6.2 Uploaded Images

  • Processing period: Images stored only during AI processing
  • Automatic deletion: Removed within 24 hours of upload
  • No long-term storage of user images

6.3 Generated Code

  • User control: Stored in account history until deleted by user
  • User deletion: Can be removed at any time
  • Account deletion: Removed when account is deleted

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update your account details
  • Delete your account and data
  • Download your data (data portability)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7.2 Communication Preferences

  • Email notifications: Can be managed in account settings
  • Marketing emails: Include unsubscribe option
  • Service emails: Cannot be disabled (security, billing)
