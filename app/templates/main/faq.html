Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to common questions about our AI-powered screenshot-to-code service

Getting Started

How do I get started?

Simply create a free account to get 3 conversion credits. Upload a screenshot, choose your framework, and our AI will generate production-ready code in seconds.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WebP formats. Maximum file size is 10MB. For best results, use high-resolution screenshots (1920x1080 or higher).

How accurate is the AI conversion?

Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy with clear, well-structured screenshots. Results depend on image quality, complexity, and clarity of the UI elements.

Frameworks & Code Output

What frameworks are supported?

We currently support:

  • React - JSX components with modern hooks
  • Vue.js - Single-file components with composition API
  • HTML/CSS/JS - Vanilla web technologies
  • Tailwind CSS - Utility-first styling
  • Bootstrap - Component-based framework

Is the generated code production-ready?

Yes! Our AI generates clean, semantic code that follows best practices including proper HTML structure, accessibility features, and responsive design principles.

Can I customize the generated code?

Absolutely! The generated code is yours to modify, enhance, and adapt to your specific needs. It serves as a solid foundation that you can build upon.

Technical Questions

How long does conversion take?

Most conversions complete in 30-60 seconds. Processing time may vary based on image complexity and current system load.

What happens to my uploaded images?

Images are automatically deleted after processing is complete (usually within 24 hours). We don't store your images permanently for privacy and security.

Can I batch process multiple screenshots?

Currently, we process one screenshot at a time. Batch processing is on our roadmap for future releases. You can convert multiple images sequentially.

Do you have an API?

API access is coming soon! We're working on developer APIs for bulk conversions and integration with design tools. Join our newsletter for updates.

Support & Troubleshooting

What if the conversion fails?

If a conversion fails due to technical issues, your credit won't be deducted. Try uploading a clearer image or contact support if the problem persists.

How do I get better results?

For optimal results:

  • Use high-resolution, clear screenshots
  • Ensure good contrast and lighting
  • Crop out unnecessary browser elements
  • Include complete UI sections

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@screenshottocode.com. We typically respond within 24 hours. Include your account email and describe the issue for faster assistance.

Can I access my conversion history?

Yes! All your conversions are saved in your account dashboard. You can re-download previous results, search by date, and manage your conversion history.
